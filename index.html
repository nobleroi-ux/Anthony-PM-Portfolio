Background

Clinical Expertise Meets Product Thinking

I am a Nigerian-trained Medical Doctor with over ten years of experience across clinical medicine, public health programming, and project management — now channelling that depth into building scalable digital health products.

My clinical career has been shaped by working in resource-constrained, high-stakes environments where solving real problems fast is not optional. That instinct — identify the bottleneck, design a fix, measure the result — is the same mindset I bring to product work.

I hold a Masters in International Public Health (in progress, EUCLID University, USA) and have completed advanced training in Leadership & Management in Health, Project Management in Global Health, and Implementation Science — all through the University of Washington.

My transition into Product Management is not a departure from medicine. It is the most direct path to solving the health system problems I've spent a decade watching hurt patients and clinicians alike.
