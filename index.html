Background

Clinical Expertise Meets Product Strategy

I am a Nigerian-trained Medical Doctor with over ten years of experience across clinical medicine, public health programming, and project management — now channeling that deep operational knowledge into building better health products.

My clinical career has been shaped by working in resource-constrained, high-stakes environments where solving real problems fast is not optional. That instinct — identifying what matters, building fast, and measuring impact — is the foundation of how I approach product work.

I hold a Masters in International Public Health (in progress) and have completed advanced training in Leadership & Management in Health from the University of Washington.

My transition into Product Management is not a departure from medicine. It is the most direct path to solving the health system problems I've spent a decade watching happen.
